Discover IceLand

Fire and Ice Wonderland

Why is it Special?

Iceland's otherworldly landscapes of volcanoes, geysers, hot springs, and glaciers make it seem like a planet of its own. The contrast between fire and ice creates scenery unlike anywhere else on Earth.

Witnessing the Northern Lights dance across the sky is at the top of my bucket list, and Iceland offers one of the best vantage points. The country's commitment to sustainability and its unique culture add even more appeal to this already magical destination.

Direct Flights

There are no direct flights from Manila to Iceland, but several airlines offer connecting flights to Keflavík International Airport (KEF), the main airport near Reykjavik. Typical routes involve one or two layovers in major international hubs.

  • Philippine Airlines + British Airways / Icelandair – Manila → London (LHR) → Reykjavik (KEF)
  • Qatar Airways + Icelandair – Manila → Doha (DOH) → Copenhagen (CPH) → Reykjavik (KEF)
  • Singapore Airlines + Lufthansa / Icelandair – Manila → Singapore (SIN) → Frankfurt (FRA) → Reykjavik (KEF)
  • Emirates + Icelandair – Manila → Dubai (DXB) → London (LHR) → Reykjavik (KEF)
  • Turkish Airlines + Play Airlines – Manila → Istanbul (IST) → Paris (CDG) → Reykjavik (KEF)

Approx. 20-30 hours, depending on layovers.
