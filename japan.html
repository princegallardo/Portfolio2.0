Discover Japan

Land of Tradition and Innovation

Why is it Special?

Japan has always fascinated me with its perfect blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology. The contrast between serene temples and bustling city streets creates a unique atmosphere that I'm eager to experience firsthand.

From the iconic Mount Fuji to the historic temples of Kyoto, Japan offers breathtaking natural landscapes alongside rich cultural heritage. The Japanese attention to detail is evident in their cuisine, architecture, and traditional arts.

What I Want to Experience

Cherry Blossoms

Witness the magical cherry blossoms in spring at Ueno Park and other famous viewing spots.

Ancient Temples

Explore the ancient temples and gardens of Kyoto, including Kinkaku-ji and Fushimi Inari.

Tokyo's Energy

Experience the vibrant energy of Tokyo's Shibuya Crossing and the city's unique districts.

Traditional Onsen

Soak in a traditional onsen hot spring for a relaxing and authentic Japanese experience.

Japanese Cuisine

Sample authentic Japanese cuisine from street food to fine dining establishments.

Cultural Immersion

Participate in a traditional tea ceremony or try your hand at calligraphy.

Get Your Guide

Direct Flights

Several airlines offer direct flights from Manila to major Japanese cities:

  • Philippine Airlines: Direct flights to Tokyo (Narita and Haneda), Osaka, Nagoya, and Fukuoka
  • Japan Airlines: Direct flights to Tokyo (Narita and Haneda)
  • All Nippon Airways (ANA): Direct flights to Tokyo (Haneda)
  • Cebu Pacific: Direct flights to Tokyo (Narita), Osaka, and Nagoya

Flight duration: Approximately 4-5 hours depending on the destination
