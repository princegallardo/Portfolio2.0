Discover Peru

Ancient Civilizations and Natural Wonders

Why is it Special?

Peru's rich history and archaeological treasures have long fascinated me. The mysterious Inca civilization and their engineering marvels, particularly Machu Picchu, represent one of humanity's greatest achievements.

Beyond the ancient ruins, Peru offers incredible biodiversity from the Amazon rainforest to the Andes mountains. The country's vibrant culture and renowned cuisine make it a multifaceted destination that appeals to all my interests.

What I Want to Experience

Machu Pichu

The world-famous Incan citadel.

Lake Titicaca

Visit the Uros floating islands and Taquile Island.

Take a Pisco Winery Tour

Visit vineyards in Ica and learn about Peru’s national drink.

Beaches in Máncora

Relax on Peru’s best Pacific coastline with warm waters.

Swim with Pink Dolphins

In the Amazon River near Iquitos.

Bosque de Piedra (Stone Forest)

A little-known but fascinating rock formation in Junín.

Direct Flights

There are no direct flights from Manila to Peru, but several airlines offer connecting flights to Jorge Chávez International Airport (LIM) in Lima, Peru. Most routes have one or two layovers, usually in the U.S., Europe, or Middle East.

  • Philippine Airlines + LATAM Airlines – Manila → Los Angeles (LAX) → Lima (LIM)
  • United Airlines – Manila → San Francisco (SFO) → Houston (IAH) → Lima (LIM)
  • Qatar Airways + LATAM Airlines – Manila → Doha (DOH) → São Paulo (GRU) → Lima (LIM)
  • KLM Royal Dutch Airlines – Manila → Amsterdam (AMS) → Bogotá (BOG) → Lima (LIM)
  • Emirates + Avianca – Manila → Dubai (DXB) → Madrid (MAD) → Lima (LIM)

Approx. 26-40 hours, depending on layovers.
