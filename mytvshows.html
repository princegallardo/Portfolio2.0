My Kind of Film: TV Shows

Exploring the stories that captivate my imagination and touch my heart

Anime

Japanese animation has always fascinated me with its unique storytelling, vibrant visuals, and emotional depth. Here are some of my favorite anime series and films that have left a lasting impression.

Solo Leveling

A-1 Pictures
Taito Ban, Genta Nakamura

Sung Jin-woo, a weak E-rank hunter, discovers a secret system that allows him to level up and become incredibly powerful by completing quests and battling monsters solo, transforming him from the weakest to one of the strongest hunters.

Action Fantasy Adventure

Sakamoto Days

TMS Entertainment
Akio Otsuka, Kenjiro Tsuda

Taro Sakamoto, a legendary, retired assassin who now runs a convenience store with his family, struggling to balance his past with his present while facing threats from his former world.

Action Comedy Slice of Life

One Piece

Toei Animation
Mayumi Tanaka, Kazuya Nakai

Monkey D. Luffy's journey to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure, "One Piece," after eating a Devil Fruit, granting him rubber-like abilities, and assembling a crew of talented misfits.

Adventure Fantasy Comedy

K-Drama

Korean dramas have captured my heart with their compelling characters, intricate plots, and perfect balance of humor, romance, and emotional depth. These are some of my favorite K-dramas that I've enjoyed watching.

Squid Game

Hwang Dong-hyuk
Lee Jung-jae, Park Hae-soo

A group of people who participate in deadly games for a huge cash prize. The show is based on Korean children's games and draws on the creator's own economic struggles.

Thriller Drama Survival

The Trauma Code (Heroes On The Call)

Yoo In-shik
Lee Dong-wook, Shin Hyun-been

Dr. Baek Kang-hyuk, a genius trauma surgeon, revitalizes a struggling trauma unit at Hankuk University Hospital, facing both medical challenges and bureaucratic obstacles while saving lives.

Medical Drama Action

The Uncanny Counter

Yoo Sun-dong
Jo Byeong-gyu, Yoo Jun-sang

A group of demon hunters, disguised as noodle shop employees, uses their unique abilities to combat evil spirits that have escaped the afterlife and prey on humans.

Fantasy Action Supernatural
