Discover New Zealand

Breathtaking Landscapes

Why is it Special?

New Zealand's diverse and dramatic landscapes have captured my imagination ever since I saw them showcased in films. From lush forests to snow-capped mountains, this island nation offers some of the most spectacular natural scenery on Earth.

As an adventure enthusiast, I'm drawn to the countless outdoor activities available, from hiking and kayaking to bungee jumping. The country's commitment to preserving its natural beauty while making it accessible to visitors makes it an ideal destination for nature lovers.

Direct Flights

Several airlines operate flights from Manila to major cities in New Zealand, usually with one stop:

  • Philippine Airlines: Flights to Auckland (via connecting flights)
  • Air New Zealand: Flights to Auckland (with a stopover in Singapore or Australia)
  • Singapore Airlines: Flights to Auckland, Wellington, and Christchurch (with a stopover in Singapore)
  • Qatar Airways: Flights to Auckland (with a stopover in Doha)
  • Emirates: Flights to Auckland and Christchurch (with a stopover in Dubai)
  • Qantas Airways: Flights to Auckland, Wellington, and Christchurch (with a stopover in Sydney or Melbourne)

Flight duration: Approximately 12-16 hours, including layovers.
